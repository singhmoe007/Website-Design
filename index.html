Color

Colors play a crucial role in web design as they can impact a user's emotional response, help establish brand identity, and guide user behavior. Proper use of colors can improve user experience, enhance readability, and increase engagement. Colors can be used to create contrast, hierarchy, and emphasis, and can be used to evoke specific emotions or convey meaning. A well-chosen color palette can make a website stand out and leave a lasting impression on visitors.
